# Apas

`Apas` is the water tattwa. In the text already captured in this project, it is described as the concentrating principle and the opposite of `Tejas`.

## Core qualities

- Concentration and attraction
- Wealth, commerce, and affluence
- Art, intuition, and refined perception
- Love, sensuality, and conception
- Venusian softness rather than aggression

## Favorable actions

- Commerce and business dealings
- Buying jewelry and clothes
- Financial speculation and investments
- Artistic creation such as painting and music
- Sea travel
- Acts of charity
- Love and romantic matters

## Unfavorable tendencies

- Alcohol and excess
- Greed in less developed states

## Visual direction for animation

The animation should not feel like rain, storm, or chaos. It should feel:

- reflective
- liquid
- attractive or magnetic
- inward-flowing
- calm, sensual, and luminous

The water should look like it gathers and holds energy. Ripples can converge toward a center, highlights can shimmer softly, and motion should suggest intuition and attraction more than impact or turbulence.
