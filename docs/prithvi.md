# Prithvi

`Prithvi` is the earth tattwa. In the text already captured in this project, it is described as the tattwa of joy, health, and justice.

## Core qualities

- Health and nourishment
- Justice and right order
- Joy, well-being, and optimism
- Cohesion and solidarity
- Stability, support, and benevolence
- Jupiter as "greater fortune"

## Favorable actions

- Eating and nourishment
- Legal matters and seeking justice
- Marriages and lasting unions
- Religious practices and prayer
- Starting friendships
- Medical treatments
- Conferences and gatherings

## Unfavorable tendencies

- Very few; it is described as the most benevolent tattwa
- Less favorable during certain Mars/Saturn periods

## Visual direction for animation

The animation should not feel restless, airy, or explosive. It should feel:

- grounded
- stable
- cohesive
- nourishing
- protective
- quietly luminous

Instead of obvious movement, the earth should show slow presence: strata, rooted forms, embedded minerals, steady glow, and only slight settling or glinting. The feeling should be land that holds, supports, and resolves rather than land that drifts or erupts.
